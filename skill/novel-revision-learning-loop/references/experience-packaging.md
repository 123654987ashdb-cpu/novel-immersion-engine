# Experience Packaging

Experience packaging turns many project-specific revision lessons into reusable guidance.

## Privacy Rule

The raw revision record may include names, plot facts, spoilers, and exact lines because it stays inside the project. The packaged experience must remove or generalize:

- Character names.
- Unique setting names.
- Plot spoilers.
- Exact proprietary passages unless the author explicitly wants them kept.
- One-off facts that cannot transfer.

## Packaging Rules

- Prefer principles with evidence over slogans.
- Keep each lesson actionable.
- Preserve boundaries and反例 so the pack does not become rigid.
- Merge duplicate lessons instead of growing the pack forever.
- Retire lessons that later author edits contradict.
- Feed `整体创作经验包` to the planning skill and `文笔执行经验包` to the prose renderer.
